Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Overview

Ham Radio Study Buddy is designed as an on-device study app. The app stores study progress, bookmarks, completed lessons, settings, and purchase status locally on your device.

Information We Collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information for app sign-in. Purchase transactions are handled by Apple through the App Store.

Local App Data

Study progress and preferences are stored locally so the app can show your history, streaks, review schedule, and saved questions.

Purchases

Premium unlocks are processed through Apple in-app purchase systems. Restore Purchase checks App Store entitlements for the Apple Account currently signed in on the device.

Ham Radio Study Buddy Support

Help and contact information for Ham Radio Study Buddy, an exam study app for the FCC Amateur Radio Technician license.

Contact

For support, purchase questions, or feedback, email jdelvo06@gmail.com.

Premium Purchase Help

  • Use the in-app Restore Purchase button if Premium does not unlock after reinstalling the app.
  • Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple Account that made the purchase.
  • If the App Store reports a successful purchase but the app remains locked, restart the app and try Restore Purchase again.

Study Progress

Progress, bookmarks, lesson completion, streaks, and review state are stored on your device. You can export or reset progress from Settings.

FCC Question Pool

Ham Radio Study Buddy is an independent study tool and is not endorsed by the FCC. Question pool material is provided for exam preparation.
